# Remote Work Policy

1. Introduction
The company supports remote work to allow employees more flexibility and better work-life balance while maintaining productivity. This policy outlines the guidelines for working remotely.

2. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their initial 90-day probationary period are eligible to apply for up to 3 days of remote work per week.

3. Equipment and Security
Employees must use company-issued laptops for all work-related activities. Personal devices are strictly prohibited for accessing proprietary corporate systems and client data. Remote workers must connect via the corporate VPN at all times.

4. Communication Expectations
Employees working remotely must be available on Microsoft Teams during core working hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time). Responses to urgent messages are expected within 30 minutes. 

5. Leave Policy Interaction
Working remotely is not a substitute for time off. If an employee is sick or unable to work, they must log a formal sick leave request in the HR portal.

# Corporate Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be requested at least two weeks in advance. 

2. Sick Leave
The company provides 12 days of paid sick leave annually. For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note must be submitted to the HR department.

3. Public Holidays
All regular national holidays are observed. If you are required to work on a public holiday, you will be granted 1.5x regular pay or a day off in lieu.
